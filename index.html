Privacy Policy — Bitanic

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Bitanic ("the app", "we", "us") is a casual plant-care game. This policy explains what data is handled when you use the app. We have tried to keep it simple and honest.

1. Data we collect ourselves

None that leaves your device for our own use. The app has no user accounts and no login. All your game progress (plants, coins, settings) is stored locally on your device and is not sent to us or to any server we control.

2. Data collected by advertising (Google AdMob)

Bitanic shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. the advertising identifier / Device ID)
  • Advertising and product-interaction data (e.g. ads viewed or tapped)
  • Crash and performance/diagnostic data

This data may be used for third-party advertising, measurement and analytics. Google's handling of this data is described in Google's Privacy Policy and Google AdMob's policies.

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On first launch the app asks for your permission to track (the iOS ATT prompt). If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized. You can change this at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4. Purchases

In-app purchases (e.g. remove ads, coins) are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple's handling is covered by Apple's own privacy policy.

5. Children

Bitanic is family-friendly and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are configured to be served in line with Google's and Apple's policies.

6. Your choices

  • Turn off ad tracking via the iOS ATT prompt or iOS Settings.
  • Purchase "Remove Ads" to stop banner ads.
  • Delete the app to remove all locally-stored game data.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "last updated" date.
